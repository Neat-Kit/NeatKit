APA 7th edition quick reference
✍️
Author format
Last, F. M. — e.g. Okonkwo, C. J. Up to 20 authors listed; 21+ use ellipsis then last author.
📅
Year in parentheses
Always directly after author: Okonkwo, C. J. (2023). Use (n.d.) when no date available.
🔤
Title capitalization
Articles and chapters: sentence case only. Books: sentence case, then italicize.
📐
Hanging indent
First line flush left; all subsequent lines indented 0.5". References alphabetized by author surname.
🔗
DOI format
Use https://doi.org/xxxxx — no "Retrieved from" needed for DOIs.
👥
et al. usage
3+ authors: use et al. from first citation (italicized). Two authors: always name both with &.
📏
Double spacing
Entire document double-spaced including references. No extra space between paragraphs. 1" margins.
🏛️
No publisher location
APA 7th removed city/country for publishers. Just: Publisher Name. No location needed.
